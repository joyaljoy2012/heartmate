Our Core Diagnostic Services

Comprehensive cardiovascular investigations performed by highly qualified Cardiac Physiologists using state-of-the-art equipment.

Echocardiogram

A detailed ultrasound scan of the heart to assess its structure, valves, and pumping function in real-time.

📈

Electrocardiogram (ECG)

A simple and fast test to evaluate the heart's electrical rhythm and detect abnormalities or ischemia.

🏃‍♂️

Exercise Stress Test

Monitoring your heart's performance while walking on a treadmill to detect coronary artery disease.

🔋

Pacemaker Interrogation

Comprehensive checks and programming of implanted pacemakers and defibrillators to ensure optimal function.

⏱️

Holter Monitoring

Continuous ECG recording for 24-hours, 72-hours, or 1-week to catch intermittent palpitations and arrhythmias.

🩺

Consultations

Expert review of all diagnostic test results with comprehensive treatment plans from top cardiologists.
